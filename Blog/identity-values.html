Strengths and limitations within learning:

A definite strength I have in regards to learning is that I will preserve. I will be patient and try to keep at it. Perseverance and patience. Keep pursuing the right answer or way, be patient with your own understanding and keep persevering if it does get super hard. Over time my confidence will grow and my perseverance will have paid off.

Culture and values:

I was brought up in a household that good behaviour was taught to us at a very early age. Luke (my brother) and I were taught at a young age that being kind, polite and using manners was the only right way of behaving. My parents, one being kiwi, and one being english, did have differences in the thoughts of raising children but they set a culture of hard work ethic and being responsible for our actions.
