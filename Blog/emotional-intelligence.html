What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is a person's ability to perceive, control and evaluate emotions. Not only their own, but being able to understand, interpret and respond to other people's emotions too. It's a known fact that emotions can change your thoughts, for instance when your emotions are running high and you are stressed, it changes the way our brain functions and it can blur our cognitive abilities and behaviour, decision making and even our interpersonal skills can be affected. Being able to understand our own emotions and change our behaviour to positively affect others emotions can help us in our personal and professional lives. Recent studies have shown that having a high EQ can boost your chances massively in being successful within getting jobs, especially within the tech industry.
