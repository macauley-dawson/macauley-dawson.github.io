-Growth Mindset-
Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

Growth mindset is the mindset of being more positive in the way you learn and problem solve so you don't lose motivation and belief that you can do it. Having a growth mindset is changing the way you think to enable you to learn and grow in a positive and productive way. It's shaping your brain to think in a different way about challenges and problem solving, enabling you to be more encouraged about learning and not doubting yourself when things get hard. It's relevant to learning, as it can change the way you take in information and retain that information. Knowing that you will eventually learn it because it is possible mentally, is growth mindset and that will help power your positive attitude towards learning.
